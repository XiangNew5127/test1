Muay Thai

The Art of Eight Limbs

Muay Thai, also known as the "Art of Eight Limbs," is a dynamic and powerful martial art originating from Thailand. It combines the use of fists, elbows, knees, and shins to deliver a full-body workout that enhances strength, agility, and endurance. Practiced by warriors for centuries, Muay Thai is not just about physical prowess; it is also about discipline, mental fortitude, and respect. Whether you're looking to improve your fitness, learn self-defense, or compete at a professional level, Muay Thai offers a rewarding path to personal growth and achievement.

Head of Trainer

Rodtang Jitmuangnon

We are proud to introduce Rodtang Jitmuangnon, the head trainer at Fitness Club's Muay Thai program. Rodtang, often referred to as "The Iron Man," is a globally recognized Muay Thai champion with an impressive record in the ring. Known for his relentless fighting style, unmatched technique, and inspiring dedication, Rodtang brings a wealth of experience and passion to our training sessions. Under his guidance, you will learn the true essence of Muay Thai, from fundamental techniques to advanced strategies, all while building the mental and physical toughness that this martial art demands.
